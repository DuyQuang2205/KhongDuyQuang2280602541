## Lessons Learned

* When deploying a system on AWS, it is important to understand the role of each service in the overall architecture.
* Auto Scaling and Load Balancer are important components for building scalable and stable systems.
* CloudWatch is essential for monitoring performance, detecting issues, and supporting system operations.
* Cost management is a critical skill when learning and working with cloud services.
* Participating in community events helps expand knowledge beyond labs, especially in emerging topics such as AI, Prompt Engineering, and AI Agents.
* In addition to technical skills, enterprise mindset is also important for interns and junior developers.
