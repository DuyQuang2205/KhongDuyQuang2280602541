* Reviewing the entire system required significant time because both the application and documentation needed verification.

## Lessons Learned

* Maintaining documentation throughout the development process greatly reduces the workload during the final stage.
* Performing multiple rounds of testing before the final demonstration helps ensure system stability.
* A technical report should accurately reflect both the implementation process and the lessons learned, not just the final product.
* Effective time management is essential when balancing development, testing, documentation, and presentation tasks.
* The internship significantly strengthened my knowledge of AWS, Docker, Cloud Computing, and the practical workflow of designing, deploying, and maintaining cloud-based applications.
