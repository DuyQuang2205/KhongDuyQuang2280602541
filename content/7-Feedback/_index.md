### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
FCAJ provides a working environment that is not only friendly but also highly professional and supportive. Team members are always ready to assist when I encounter challenges, demonstrating a spirit of collaboration that extends beyond working hours. The office is well-organized, equipped with all necessary tools and resources, creating a focused and creative workspace. To enhance this further, I suggest organizing more team-building activities, cross-departmental networking sessions, or off-site gatherings to strengthen relationships among team members.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentorship I received stands as one of the program's greatest strengths. My mentor not only provided detailed guidance and clear explanations of complex concepts but also actively encouraged me to ask questions and explore independently. Importantly, the mentor employed effective coaching methodology by allowing me to solve problems myself rather than simply providing ready-made answers. The admin team was equally proficient in handling administrative procedures, providing necessary documentation, and creating favorable conditions for me to focus on core responsibilities.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assignments I received were carefully designed to bridge my foundational knowledge from university with cutting-edge technologies I had never encountered before. Specifically, through the PetCare Shop project, I had the opportunity to apply system design principles while gaining deep insights into AWS cloud architecture, cost optimization, and best practices for production-ready systems. This structure created an optimal learning curve: challenging enough to foster growth, yet familiar enough to avoid overwhelming stress.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout my internship, I developed not only technical skills but also critical soft skills. On the technical side, I gained deeper proficiency in cloud architecture design, Infrastructure as Code principles, AI workload cost optimization strategies, and deploying production-grade applications on AWS. For soft skills, I improved my technical communication abilities, technical documentation writing, working in agile environments, and project management. My mentor also shared extensive real-world experience from previous projects, helping me understand the genuine challenges cloud engineers face and strategies to overcome them.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
FCAJ's company culture reflects a positive, professional, yet deeply human-centered work environment. Everyone respects each other regardless of hierarchy or role, and work is completed seriously while maintaining a sense of enjoyment and comfort. Particularly notable is how during tight deadlines or urgent issues, the entire team collaborates seamlessly without regard to position or department. This made me feel that I wasn't just an intern but truly a valuable, indispensable member of the team contributing to our collective mission.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
FCAJ maintains a very reasonable support policy for interns, including fair internship allowances, flexible working arrangements when needed during busy periods, and access to internal training programs free of charge. This opportunity to access the latest technologies and best practices without additional cost is a significant advantage. The feedback and evaluation mechanisms are also transparent, helping me understand my strengths, areas for improvement, and direction for future development.

### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**

The most satisfying aspect was the opportunity to work directly with real AWS services and design a cloud architecture for a meaningful project. Additionally, I was excited to participate in events and workshops about GenAI and AI cost optimization, which significantly expanded my knowledge of modern technology trends.

**What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**

I believe the company should organize more practical, real-world business scenarios so interns can gain more diverse hands-on experience.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**

Absolutely. FCAJ is an excellent place for anyone who wants to learn deeply about AWS cloud services and AI. Most importantly, interns are given meaningful work assignments, not just mundane administrative tasks. This is the ideal place to develop practical skills, build a strong portfolio, and prepare for a successful career.

### Suggestions & Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

Add more practical, real-world scenarios that businesses commonly encounter so interns can gain more diverse experience.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**

Yes, if the opportunity arises, I would love to continue the program. I feel that I have found a place where my knowledge, skills, and personal values are truly valued and nurtured.

**Any other comments (free sharing):**

I want to sincerely thank the FCAJ team for providing me with an exceptional internship experience. The experiences, skills, and people I've met here will be invaluable assets for my future career. Wishing FCAJ continued success!
